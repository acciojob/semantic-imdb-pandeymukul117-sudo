Summary

3 Idiots is a Bollywood movie that follows the lives of three engineering students. It shows the pressure of studies, the importance of friendship, and the courage to follow one's passion instead of blindly chasing success.

Actors

The movie stars Aamir Khan as Rancho, R. Madhavan as Farhan, and Sharman Joshi as Raju. Their performances made the movie entertaining and meaningful.
