Summary

3 Idiots is a story about three engineering students who question the traditional education system. The movie focuses on friendship, pressure, success, and following one's passion in life.

Actors

The main actors are Aamir Khan as Rancho, R. Madhavan as Farhan, and Sharman Joshi as Raju. Their performances made the movie memorable and impactful.
